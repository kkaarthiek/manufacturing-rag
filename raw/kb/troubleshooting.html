Troubleshooting Knowledge Base

TG-001 - Short Shot ("the squeeze didn't fill")

Hydra injection molder (MCH-302). A short shot is an incompletely filled part.

  • Melt temperature too low: check barrel zone setpoints / heater bands (a failed band caused NCR-7002 via WO-5002).
  • Injection or hold pressure too low: raise in small steps.
  • Blocked gate or vent: inspect and clean the tool.
  • Insufficient shot size: increase shot volume; check the check-ring.

TG-002 - Lathe Chatter / Vibration

Cyclops lathe (MCH-301). Watch the PRT-2005 sensor reading.

  • Worn spindle bearing (PRT-2003): root cause behind WO-5001.
  • Excessive spindle speed: reduce rpm.
  • Long tool overhang / dull insert: shorten overhang, index tool.
  • Loose workholding: re-seat and re-clamp.

If average vibration exceeds the 4.5 mm/s alarm limit, stop and investigate.
